📱 Smartphone IMU Test

Compare your phone's IMU with ESP32 sensor

⚠️ iOS Users: You need to grant motion sensor permission. Android: Works automatically in most browsers.
Make sure to open this page on your smartphone, not desktop!

📋 How to Use

  1. Click "Start Smartphone IMU" to enable phone sensors
  2. Optionally connect your ESP32 IMU via Bluetooth
  3. Hold phone and ESP32 sensor together in the same orientation
  4. Compare the Roll, Pitch, Yaw values between both
  5. The "Difference" card shows how much they differ
